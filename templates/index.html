Education Spending in Different Regions

Data Pipeline Project Byte1

Guiding Question

As we all know, educational resources are unevenly distributed between different regions all around the world. Education spending is one of the key factors to evaluate one nations' education resources. In this project byte, I hope to learn about how nations in different regions (e.g, Western and Estern) differ in education spending.

Method

To answer my question, I used an open dataset from theguardian.com that has been published by Pisa and The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD). The dataset provides education spending data from 38 countries all around the world. I think this dataset can be effective to answer my question. First, I want to make a general, descriptive comparison between countries. Then, I want to know how different countries spend their money on specific areas such as teacher's salary and students. Moreover, I hope to explore a little bit more on the relationship between the public and private investments on education. During the data analysis (mostly descriptive) and visualization process, I used google fusion table and spreadsheets
